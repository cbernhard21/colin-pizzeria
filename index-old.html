Our Story

Colin's Pizzeria is nested within the north side Chicago neighborhood of Andersonville. Established in 2015, Colin's has been a favorite among locals. We use locally grown ingredients (whenever possible). Groups of all sizes can be accommodated. So whether you are meeting friends to watch the game or just looking for a night out, Colin's is the place to enjoy good food and spirits.
